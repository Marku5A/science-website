Sensors


Water Level Sensor:

Mesures the level of water that it's submerged in, and if it's above a certain amount, it sets of the alarm.

Could be used to sense the begining of floods or water leaks.


IR Motion Sensor:

Senses motion in front of it and sets of the alarm.

Coul be used for a motion controlled light or alarm system.


DHT11 Humidity and Temperature Sensor:

Mesures the humidity and temperature of the air around it.

Could be used as part of a thermostat or hygrometer.
