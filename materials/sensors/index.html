Sensors


Water Level Sensor:

Measures the level of water that it's submerged in, and if it's above a certain amount, it sets of the alarm.

Could be used to sense the beginning of floods or water leaks.


IR Motion Sensor:

Senses motion in front of it and sets of the alarm.

Could be used for a motion controlled light or alarm system.


DHT11 Humidity and Temperature Sensor:

Measures the humidity and temperature of the air around it.

Could be used as part of a thermostat or hygrometer.


MQ-2 Gas Sensor:

Senses the approximate concentration of H2, LPG, CH4, CO, Alcohol, Smoke or Propane.

Could be used as a gas leakage sensor or as part of a smoke detector.


Flame Sensor:

Senses the proximity of a flame.

Could be used as part of a fire sensor.


Photoresistor:

Senses how much light there is and if the light level is low enough it turns on 2 white LED's.

Could be used for automatic outdoor lights which turn on at night.


Colour Sensor:

Senses the approximate RGB values of an object in front of it.

No real use in an actual house
