Indicators


Red LED:

If one of the sensors activates, it flashes the led for a certain amount of time.

Could be used for a visual indication of an alarm.


Buzzer:

If one of the sensors activates, it beeps.

Could be used for an auditory indication of an alarm


Two White LED's

If the light around the photoresistor is below a certain amount, they turn on.

Could be used as outside lights that turn on at night.
